H — Narrative Case Summary
H.1 Case Narrative
An 81-year-old female patient received Mevalotin (pravastatin sodium) 20mg for hyperlipidemia since July 2008. The patient experienced interstitial pneumonia on 08 October 2008 (inpatient hospitalization).
H.2 Reporter's Comments
Although the results of DLSTs were all negative, drug-induced pneumonia was most considered from the onset of interstitial pneumonia, the condition of the shadows and the clinical course.
H.4 Sender's Comments
This is a spontaneously received case reported by a literature that is categorized as serious due to inpatient hospitalization. Daiichi Sankyo's assessment of the causal relationship between interstitial pneumonia and Mevalotin is related.
